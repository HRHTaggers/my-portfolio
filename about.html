About me

Background

I started out my career in the world of fine chocolate, running operations for some of the most talented chocolatiers in London. After 8 delicious years and a couple of stone later, I decided to move into FMCG and started working for MarsWrigley as part of their Sales team, dealing with both external and internal stakeholders.

After taking part in a Hackathon at MW, I was bitten by the coding bug and decided to start learning code, with a view to building business-specific apps.

Coding

As a humanities graduate of the University of St Andrews, my first love has always been language and literature. Coding is the next step in my exploration of the nature of language - human/machine communication.

I started coding with SheCodes, completing their Basic, Plus and Responsive courses whilst on maternity leave. This gave me chance to develop skills in the following areas:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JS5
  • Bootstrap
  • Github
  • Hosting
  • APIs
  • Code Editors
  • Flexbox

To discover more about my current portfolio, please explore my gitHub repositories in the footer link.

I am currently following the SheCodes React course and continuing my coding adventures with Udemy and Dataquest.
